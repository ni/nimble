An accordion item is a collapsible section that can be expanded or collapsed
to show or hide its content. It uses a header with an expander icon to toggle
visibility.

## Appearance

Use the story controls on the primary canvas to switch between `ghost`, `outline`,
and `block` appearances.

`ghost` is the default and works well for lightweight or nested content.

`outline` adds a divider between grouped items.

`block` gives each summary row a filled background for settings-style layouts.
